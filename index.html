FAQ

What is mySpotLight?
mySpotLight is a photo editor for iPhone, iPad and any Android device. You can spotlight your images with a few fingertipps and share them instantly on Facebook and Twitter, e-mail them to your friends or send them to your dropbox.
How does it work?
Spotlighting is very fast and easy. You roughly mark the parts you want to highlight. Afterwards you can adjust the amount of Spotlight and Focus with two sliders.
Why is spotlighting so easy?
The algorithms cooking inside the app are powered by artificial intelligence. Even if you mark your desired image parts very roughly - the app instantly "knows" what you want to highlight.
What is mySpotLight NOT?
mySpotLight is NOT just another photo editor with a set of fancy filter effects. It is only designed for one thing: Highlighting the important parts in your image and blinding out the rest.
